Hi there, I'm Alberto Alvarez!

I am an Artificial Intelligence and Games researcher mainly focusing on exploring game design through Human-AI collaborative approaches. I am currently Assistant Professor at Malmö University, Sweden, where I research on the collaboration between Humans and AI to co-create and co-design games and creative artifacts! I mainly focus on the use of AI as a way to enhance human capabilities and to foster creativity in ideation and creative processes. This website is still under construction!
